About

Photographer | Entrepreneur | Visionary


Born in Ohio and raised in Sacramento, Jacob earned his degree in Construction Management Technology from University of Maryland Eastern Shore in 2013. Having to take images everyday for work, Jacob soon found his passion for photography in different scenes of beauty on the site. Already having an appreciation for the aesthetics of architecture, photography was a talent that came to him naturally. After years of self teaching and studying, Jacob has already made an impact for local businesses in the Greater Washington Area.
